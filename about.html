Who is ATEEZ?

Ateez is an 8-member South Korean Boy group managed by KQ Entertainment. The group consists of: Hongjoong, Seonghwa, Yunho, Yeosang, San, Mingi, Wooyoung, and Jongho.

They debuted on October 24th, 2018 with the songs ‘Treasure’ and ‘Pirate King’. They recently released a new album called, 'ZERO: FEVER Part 3', which was released on September 13, 2021.
